# Liquid

Free self-service open access to download, customize, and deploy models (via Hugging Face or LEAP platform) under the LFM Open License v1.0, which has a commercial use threshold: free for companies under $10 million in annual revenue; companies above this must purchase a commercial license. Liquid Apollo is also free for mobile devices. Enterprise tiers offer custom agreements.
