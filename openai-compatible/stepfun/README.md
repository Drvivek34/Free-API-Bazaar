# StepFun

Offers the Step series of multimodal models. Free tier is available under the V0 tier ($0 account balance) with rate limits of 10 RPM and 5,000,000 TPM. Certain models (like Step 3.5 Flash) are also free to use via OpenRouter. Paid tiers require topping up your account balance (starting from $15 up to $1,500+), which increases RPM, TPM, and concurrency limits.
